Priming Prompts are automatically included at the beginning of every conversation with the AI. They establish context and guide the AI's behavior throughout the entire conversation.

Unlike one-time prompts that are used only for a single message, priming prompts shape the entire chat experience.

You can add any of your prompts or favorites as priming prompts, and arrange them in order of importance.
